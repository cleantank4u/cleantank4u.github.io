Our Certified 6-Stage Mechanized Cleaning Method

1

Manhole Access

Safe entry & preparation of the tank for cleaning.

2

Dewatering & De-sludging

High-powered pumps remove residual water and bottom sludge.

3

High-Pressure Jet

Internal walls, ceiling, and base are scrubbed using professional high-pressure jets.

4

Vacuum Extraction

Industrial wet vacuum cleaners remove all dirt, debris, and dirty water.

5

Anti-Bacterial Treatment

Eco-friendly, food-grade disinfectant sprayed to sterilize inner surfaces.

6

UV Sterilization (Key Feature)

Final treatment with UV radiation to kill air- and water-borne bacteria & viruses.
